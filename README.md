### 👨‍💻 About Me  

I'm **Dr. Arun Kumar**, a passionate technologist with over **8 years of experience in coding and AI-driven problem solving**.  
Starting my journey as a **Full Stack Developer**, I transitioned into **Machine Learning and Data Science**, where I now design intelligent, production-grade systems.  

I’ve recently been awarded my **Ph.D.**, and my research focuses on **applied machine learning** and **data-driven systems**.  
I’ve also served as a **Teaching Assistant for 2 years**, published **multiple research papers**, and was honored with a **Gold Medal** during my post-graduation.  

> I love bridging the gap between research and real-world applications — whether it’s model deployment, AI engineering, or end-to-end ML pipelines.
